543.050
NOTES OF DECISIONS
State has no power to veto a license granted by
Federal Government to construct a dam across a
navigable stream for purposes of generating electricity.
State v. Idaho Power Co., (1957) 211 Or 284, 312 P2d 583.
Federal Power Commission had exclusive jurisdic-
tion to grant license for water power project on lands
in Oregon constituting United States reservation. Fed-
eral Power Comm. v. Oregon, (1955) 349 US 435, 444, 75
S Ct 832, 838, 99 L Ed 1215, 1224.
FURTHER CITATIONS: Ollio v. Clatskanie Dist., (1942)
170 Or 173, 132 P2d 416.

on streams withdrawn from appropriations, 1930-32, p
240; procedure for approval of the construction of a
hydroelectric dam, 1948-50, p 252.

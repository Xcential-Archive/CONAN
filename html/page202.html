541.947 Watershed Conservation
Grant Fund. (1) The Watershed Conserva-
tion Grant Fund is established separate and
distinct from the General Fund. The Water-
shed Conservation Grant Fund shall consist
of all moneys placed in the fund as provided
by law. All moneys in the Watershed Con-
servation Grant Fund are continuously ap-
propriated to the Oregon Watershed
Enhancement Board for grants to fund proj-
ects described in ORS 541.958 that use grant
moneys as provided under ORS 541.956. In-
terest accruing to the Watershed Conserva-
tion Grant Fund shall be credited to the
fund. Watershed Conservation Grant Fund
moneys appropriated and not expended by
the completion of a biennium shall remain in
the Watershed Conservation Grant Fund.
(2) The board also may accept gifts and
grants from any public or private source for
the purpose of providing the grants described
in this section. [2011 c.643 §12]

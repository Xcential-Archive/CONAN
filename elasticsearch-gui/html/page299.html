NOTES OF DECISIONS
When applicant for a permit to construct water
reservoirs filed its application and objectors filed pro-
tests with State Engineer, hearing was held, order re-
jecting application made, applicant served notice of
appeal and filed transcript with circuit court and State
Engineer certified exhibits and transcripts, the proce-
dure was sufficient to give the court jurisdiction to re-
examine the issues, subject to certain limitations.
Warner Valley Stock Co. v. Lynch, (1959) 215 Or 523, 336
